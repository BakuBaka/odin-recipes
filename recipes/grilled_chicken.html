Grilled Chicken Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Fresh lemon Juice
  • Paprika
  • Hot Chili Powder
  • Minced Garlic
  • Chopped Fresh Ginger
  • Salt
  • Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Stir spices, lemon juice, and salt together. Rub chicken and let marinate
  2. Preheat grill to medium high heat. Lightly oil grates.
  3. Place chicken on grill. Let cook for 30 minutes.
